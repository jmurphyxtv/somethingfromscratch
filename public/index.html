What's a Mage?
Mages are personalized collections of pages. Mages can be about any topic.
What was the inspiration for Bake A Mage?
We here at BakeAMage.com felt that the current leader of online social networking- Facebook leaves much to be desired. Many people who are frequent users of the site do not feel like it accurately represents who they are. We wanted to create a platform for these types to have the freedom to be creative with their voice in a way that avoided the feeling of "permanance".
